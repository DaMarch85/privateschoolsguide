About Private Schools Guide

Private Schools Guide is designed as an independent local guide for parents researching UK private schools. The Bath hub is the first fully built example in this starter.

Editorial position

The aim is to help parents compare local options without pretending to be an official school website. Core listings are editorial. Paid placements are kept separate and clearly labelled.

This is the structure you can scale into additional affluent local markets after Bath.

What the site already includes

  • Brand homepage and Bath hub
  • Bath directory, compare page, fees, bursaries, admissions, and open days pages
  • School profile pages for the current Bath spreadsheet list
  • Advertise and claim-a-profile pages
