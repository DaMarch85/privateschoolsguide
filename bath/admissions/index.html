Bath private school admissions

Use this page as a practical parent guide. It should sit above the school pages and help parents understand the process before they click out to individual admissions offices.

1. Build a shortlist

Start with the Bath directory, then compare age range, day or boarding mix, gender mix, fee band, and location.

2. Visit early

Use the open-days page to keep track of current dates and link directly to official admissions teams.

3. Ask about bursaries

Send parents from this page to the bursaries guide and then onward to each official school site for the precise rules.
