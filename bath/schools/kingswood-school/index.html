Parents Like

  • Boarding is available without making the whole school feel boarding-led.
  • There is real sixth-form breadth, especially across sciences, geography and economics.
  • The Lansdown campus gives it a distinctive country-edge feel for Bath.

How the School Describes Itself

  • Kingswood tends to present itself as grounded, rounded and outward-looking.
  • Its messaging blends academic seriousness with character, community and co-curricular breadth.
  • The school often foregrounds its Methodist foundation without feeling narrowly confessional.
