Parents Like

  • Families often like the girls-only senior environment combined with all-through continuity.
  • The sixth form still offers real breadth despite the school’s more focused feel.
  • Boarding is there if needed, but the school still reads primarily as a Bath day option.

How the School Describes Itself

  • The school frames itself as confident, empowering and academically serious.
  • Its own tone is about ambitious girls, individual character and a strong sense of belonging.
  • It also leans on the wider GDST network as part of its offer.
