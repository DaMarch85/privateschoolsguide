Leavers destinations

Kingswood Prep is the linked junior school within the Kingswood Foundation. Published inspection material says most pupils transfer to Kingswood Senior School.

Scholarships

Published inspection material says several prep leavers have secured scholarships in music, design and technology, drama and sport.

Inspection snapshot

In the May 2024 ISI report, the prep school met the independent school standards, with inspectors highlighting a broad curriculum, good progress and strong safeguarding. The same inspection found that the separately registered early-years childcare provision, The Garden, did not consistently meet all childcare requirements.

Assessment approach

Kingswood says it tracks progress through annual national standardised tests in English (PTE), mathematics (PTM) and general ability (CATs), plus a termly NGRT reading test and half-termly internal English and maths assessments.

Parents like

  • Families often value the direct link into Kingswood Senior School.
  • It offers a genuine prep-school atmosphere without needing boarding.
  • The Lansdown setting feels separate from the city while remaining close to Bath.

How the school describes itself

  • The school describes itself as part of the wider Kingswood Foundation rather than a standalone prep.
  • It places emphasis on breadth, confidence and a rounded junior-school experience.
  • Published material also leans into continuity with the senior school.
