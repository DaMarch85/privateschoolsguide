Parents Like

  • The hilltop setting and boarding mix make it feel distinct from central Bath schools.
  • Families often value the balance between academics, pastoral care and co-curricular life.
  • Its A-level mix is notably broad rather than narrowly specialist.

How the School Describes Itself

  • Prior Park tends to describe itself as academically ambitious, pastoral and values-led.
  • Its own language links scholarship with service, kindness and confidence.
  • The school also places visible weight on its Catholic identity and sense of community.

Bursaries

Prior Park offers means-tested bursarial support to broaden access for eligible families. Families must be registered before applying, and the bursary application must be submitted before a place is offered; a bursary can also be added to a scholarship. Awards are reviewed each year.
