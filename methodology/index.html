Methodology

How schools are listed and compared

The Bath pages in this starter are built from the Bath master spreadsheet. Profile pages should be reviewed and updated before publishing live.

1. Core directory

Every qualifying school can be included in the core local directory. Basic inclusion should stay editorial rather than paid.

2. Profile fields

Profiles focus on facts parents actually compare: ages, gender mix, day or boarding, indicative fees, bursaries, and official links.

3. Paid visibility

Featured placements, enhanced profiles, and local partner slots must be labelled clearly as featured or sponsored.

Publishing rules

  • Keep editorial summaries in your own words.
  • Do not imply official affiliation with a school.
  • Verify open day dates, fees, and bursary details against the current official sources before publishing.
  • Mark paid placements clearly.
