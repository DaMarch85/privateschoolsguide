How we list and compare schools

School pages are built from publicly available information, including official school websites, inspection material, published fee tables and other open sources. We summarise and paraphrase that material into a parent-friendly format rather than reproducing prospectuses or marketing copy in full.

Comparison tables and profile tiles are intended as a guide, not as definitive admissions advice. Fees, bursaries, open days and subject data can change, so important decisions should always be checked against the school’s own current information.

As the site grows, we will keep editorial content separate from any paid placements and clearly label sponsored features where they appear.
