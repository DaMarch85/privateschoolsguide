Privacy policy

The Private School Guide is a simple editorial website. We do not currently require user accounts. If you contact us by email, we use the information you send only to respond to your enquiry and to manage the site.

If forms, newsletter sign-ups, school-claim requests or analytics tools are added in future, this page will be updated so it explains what data is collected, why it is collected, and whether any third parties receive it.

If you would like us to remove an email or other personal information you have sent directly, please contact us at hello@privateschoolsguide.co.uk.
